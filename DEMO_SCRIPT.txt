[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's walk through the architecture diagram to see how this works behind the scenes.
First, the user's query enters the Security Checkpoint node, which scrubs any PII like email addresses and screens for prompt injection threats.
Once validated, the request routes to the Orchestrator node.
The Orchestrator acts as our supervisor and delegates to two specialized sub-agents: the Competitor Intel Agent and the Customer Health Agent.
Both sub-agents connect directly to our local FastMCP Server, which acts as our database integration to retrieve real-time competitor pricing and customer usage trends.
Next, these reports flow into the Revenue Risk Agent, which calculates the annualized financial exposure.
The strategist agent then proposes a custom retention plan, like a discount or strategic CS call.
If the strategist proposes a discount above 20 percent, the workflow hits a Human Approval Pause node.
Once approved, the final report is compiled by the Executive Summary Agent and logged into the CRM.
